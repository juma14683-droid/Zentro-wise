# Digital Skills Training Platform

A professional digital platform focused on equipping individuals with **practical, in-demand digital skills** for the modern economy. The website serves as an information hub, learning resource, and showcase for digital services and training programs.

## Overview
This platform is designed to support:
- Students and beginners entering the digital space
- Freelancers and entrepreneurs seeking skill development
- Organizations and individuals looking for digital solutions

The content emphasizes **practical application, simplicity, and real-world relevance**.

## Core Digital Skills Covered
- Graphic Design & Visual Branding  
- Social Media Management & Content Strategy  
- Web Design (Static Websites)  
- Content Creation & Copywriting  
- Freelancing & Online Work Skills  
- AI-Powered Tools for Productivity and Education  

## Technologies & Tools
- HTML5  
- CSS3  
- GitHub Pages (Free and reliable hosting)
